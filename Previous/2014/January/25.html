How the brain navigates


25 January 2014

Anthony Mcgregor from Durham University will be talking to us about his work on the science of spatial learning and navigation.

For our activity, we will be learning about other forms of navigation, such as in technology (GPS and Galileo) and other animals (including heat-seeking snakes).
