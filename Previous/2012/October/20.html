The chemistry of light

   


20 October 2012

This week Café Scientifique will host Professor Andrew Beeby as he talks about how light works and how his photochemistry research and how it relates to many other sciences and technology, such as digital screens and medicine. Through conversations with Andrew, I know he has the rare talent of communicating the difficult area of chemistry in a readily-understandable way. As such, I encourage all to attend with even a passing interest - the invaluable gift Café Scientifique offers to each of its unique friends is the chance to understand and contribute to discussions about scientific issues.

As usual there will be a lighter second half giving us the chance, along with the break, to continue to discuss science in a friendly and all-inclusive way.
